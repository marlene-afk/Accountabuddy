Why Choose AccountaBuddy?

  • Goal Setting & Tracking

    Our goal setting and tracking features allows for students to set clear, achievable goals and track your progress in real-time. Each milestone tracked is on step closer to graduation.

    We provide the opportunity to be matched with the perfect accountability partner, a student with likeminded goals who will help keep you motivated.

    Our Simple charts show streaks, completion, and long-term progress, allowing you to build up a portfolio while also acknowledging your accomplishments.

    We encourage you to set goals around sleep, breaks, and self-care for balance amidst academics.

  • Monthly Career & Graduation Check-ins

    We have implemented several check-in capabilities so that you can track internships, deadlines, and credit hours with monthly reviews to keep everything in one place.

  • Community Support

    Join a community of goal-getter students who support each other.
